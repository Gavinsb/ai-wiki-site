America’s AI Action Plan

Source URL: https://www.whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Americas-AI-Action-Plan.pdf

Publisher: The White House

Date: 2025-07

Captured: 2026-04-19

Type: PDF

Extracted notes

Core thesis

The plan frames AI as a strategic race for economic, military, and geopolitical dominance. Its stated objective is for the United States to achieve and maintain global AI leadership by accelerating innovation, building infrastructure, and leading internationally in diplomacy and security.

Key excerpts

  • “The United States is in a race to achieve global dominance in artificial intelligence (AI).”
  • “We need to build and maintain vast AI infrastructure and the energy to power it.”
  • AI should complement Americans’ work rather than replace it.
  • Federal procurement should favor systems described as objective and free from top-down ideological bias.

Main pillars

  1. Accelerate AI Innovation
  2. Build American AI Infrastructure
  3. Lead in International AI Diplomacy and Security

Dominant themes

  • deregulation and removal of barriers viewed as slowing AI progress
  • worker-first rhetoric paired with productivity and retraining claims
  • ideological neutrality and free-speech framing in federal procurement
  • national security, export controls, and geopolitical competition
  • data centers, chips, and energy expansion as core state priorities
  • exporting the American AI stack to allies while restricting adversaries
