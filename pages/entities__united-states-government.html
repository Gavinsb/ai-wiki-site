United States Government

Summary

In this source, the U.S. government frames AI policy as a strategic contest for national power, with emphasis on deregulation, infrastructure buildout, energy supply, export controls, and international AI leadership.

What this source adds

  • AI is treated as an economic and military competition in which ecosystem scale matters.
  • The policy center of gravity is national competitiveness and dominance rather than broad democratic co-design.
  • Infrastructure, chips, data centers, and power generation are elevated to first-order policy priorities.

Themes in this source

  • deregulation to accelerate AI innovation
  • infrastructure and energy expansion
  • federal procurement leverage
  • ideological neutrality framing
  • diplomacy, export control, and geopolitical competition
