Anthropic may have closed the revenue gap on OpenAI. Here's what it means for their IPOs

Source: https://www.reuters.com/technology/artificial-intelligence/openai-versus-anthropic-what-revenue-race-means-their-ipos-2026-04-08/

Captured: 2026-04-20

Original date: 2026-04-08

Summary

Reuters reported that Anthropic's annualized revenue had surpassed $30 billion, appearing to exceed OpenAI's disclosed $24 billion annualized revenue. Reuters emphasized that token-heavy enterprise workloads, especially coding, can matter more for monetization than raw user count.

Key points

  • Latest disclosed annualized revenue:

- Anthropic: $30B+

- OpenAI: $24B (from $2B/month)

  • Earlier benchmarks cited by Reuters:

- Start of 2025: OpenAI $6B, Anthropic $1B

- Early 2026: OpenAI $20B, Anthropic $9B

  • Reuters says Anthropic's growth was helped by enterprise uptake of Claude and Claude Code.
  • Reuters also notes revenue figures may not be perfectly comparable because Anthropic and OpenAI may report on different gross/net bases.

Relevance to wiki

This source provides the clearest over-time revenue proxy for comparing OpenAI and Anthropic commercial traction, while also illustrating why usage share and revenue share can diverge.
